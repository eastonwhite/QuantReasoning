Part A

Individually, your first task is to draw a flow diagram of the drivers that might affect opinions on coral reef conservation. You need to consider the different components of the system and how they relate to one another. Be ready to explain the mechanisms behind connecting the different components. You can do this by hand on paper or by using online software like http://www.mentalmodeler.org/#home. Keep in mind that you will need to draw several flow diagrams throughout this assignment.

With your teammates, discuss your individual flow diagrams and come up with a consensus flow diagram as a team.

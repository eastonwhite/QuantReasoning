Part 2

So far, your modeling efforts have focused heavily on the ecological (i.e. biology of coral reefs) dynamics. However, the social dynamics (i.e. human influences) are clearly important in this system. Further, human conservation actions are dynamic and therefore change over time.

Team up with a group in the class that focused more on the social dynamics in part 1 of this assignment. You now have…:

  1. Describe the ecological aspects of the system to the other team. You should help them understand the different components that are integral to the ecology and what outputs and insights come from your model.

  2. Build consensus model that combines the ecological and social models together.

Part B

So far, your modeling efforts have focused heavily on the social (i.e. conservation opinion) dynamics. However, the ecological dynamics (i.e. coral reef and parrotfish) are clearly important in this system. Further, coral reef cover and parrotfish abundance are dynamic and therefore change over time.

Team up with a group in the class that focused more on the ecological dynamics in part A of this assignment. With this other team, address the following questions:

  1. Describe the social aspects of the system to the other team. You should help them understand the different components that are integral conservation opinion and what outputs and insights come from your model. Have the other team describe the ecological aspects of the system in depth.

  2. Build a consensus flow diagram that combines the ecological and social models together.

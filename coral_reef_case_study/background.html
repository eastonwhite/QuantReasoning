Background

If you take the plunge at dive sites in the Caribbean, you can observe coral reefs seemingly teeming with fish. But you would be mistaken for thinking these reefs were healthy.

World renowned marine biologist, Dr. Sylvia Earle, has been exploring and making discoveries in the ocean over several decades. In 1972, marine biologist Dr. Earle wrote that in the Caribbean “tropical reefs, notable for their dazzling profusion of animal life, are almost devoid of conspicuous plants.”1 Jackson 2014 https://s.si.edu/334Htsg. This is not the case today. Watch the short video linked below (https://www.youtube.com/embed/vNjzhtEGJNw) to learn more about the current state of Caribbean coral reefs.
